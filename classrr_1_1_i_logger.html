◆ Level

enum rr::ILogger::Level

Logging levels used to filter which messages are displayed on the console or written to the log file.

Enumerator
kTrace 

Trace level used to log very specific details Some suggestions of trace level details might include:

  • Memory allocations/deallocations
  • Low level function execution
  • Very repetitive or verbose details
kDebug 

Debug level used to log general details related to development Some suggestions of debug level details might include:

  • Variable values at specific points in time
  • Successful low level operations
kInfo 

Info level used to log general details related to normal operation Some suggestions of info level details might include:

  • Successful high level operations
kWarning 

Warning level used to log unexpected behavior that may or may not affect operation.

kError 

Error level used to log unsuccessful operation that may be resolved gracefully.

kCritical 

Critical level used to log unsuccessful operation that may not be resolved gracefully.
